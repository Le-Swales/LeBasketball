How to have iq like lebron

- First step into getting an IQ like LeBron James is to study the rule book for basketball so you know every rule and updated ones.

- The next step is to study the opponent you face.

- Find your opponent’s strengths and weakness so it is easier to play against them.

- Lastly is to have very good decision making, whether it is to set up a play to score or to stop the opposing team from scoring.

- Studying the game, teams and players like homework from school, is what you need to do, to have a high IQ like Lebron James.

- LeBron calculates everything he does. Before he assists a teammate, attacks the basket, or shoots a jumper, he analyses to see which will be the most effective and efficient or which will expose his opponent’s defence.
