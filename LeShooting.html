How to shoot like Lebron James

- He has a high shooting arc. Which is letting your dominant shooting arm arch out higher and out than the guiding hand.

- The pathway of the ball goes from the middle of your legs then follows through to the middle of the chest then finishes just above your forehead.

- Letting your body frame be still, but not stiff, making it easier for you to feel the flow of your ball pathway.

- The flick at the end of his jump shot is important as it helps your shooting with 2 things, one being that it helps you shoot further away and two, helps with accuracy

STATS TO SHOW THAT HIS SHOOTING PERCENTAGE IS VERY HIGH.

In fact all in the positives meaning that he is so efficient that in a game would usually make more than half his shots.

• Career field-goal accuracy: 50.5%

• Career free-throw shooting: 73.4%

• His career Effective Field Goal Percentage Effective Field Goal percentage is an advanced stat that measures actual point production.

Example: shooting 4/10 in 3s is more productive than shooting 4/10 in 2s, which is more productive than shooting 4/10 in free-throws. More: 54.5%

• Career True Shooting Percentage True shooting percentage is an advanced stat that measures overall accuracy of free throw shooting, 2-point shooting, and 3-point shooting. More: 58.8%
